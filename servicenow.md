# ServiceNow

## Overview
ServiceNow is a cloud-based platform that supports IT service management, IT operations management, and workflow automation.

## Relevance to AIOps
ServiceNow provides AI-powered event correlation, incident management, and workflow automation that can improve visibility and reduce manual effort in hybrid IT environments.

## Key Features
- ITSM and ITOM capabilities
- Predictive intelligence
- Event correlation
- Automated workflows
- Integration with cloud and on-prem systems

## Business Relevance
ServiceNow is relevant for financial services organizations that require secure, auditable, and scalable service operations.

## Notes
Strong enterprise fit. May require more planning, budget, and change management than lighter solutions.
